Concept

Machines Listening to Themselves is a distributed audiovisual installation that transforms industrial assembly processes into a self-organizing sonic ecosystem. Mechanical actions such as vibration, airflow, electrical activity and structural resonance are captured through microphones and industrial contact sensors inside a manufacturing environment. Custom software and AI-assisted analysis translate these signals into generative sound synthesis and evolving visual structures. The system forms a distributed network where machines listen to their own processes while responding to environmental and operational data streams.
